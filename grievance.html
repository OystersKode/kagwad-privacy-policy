Grievance Redressal Policy

1. Introduction

Kagwad.in ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to ensuring a safe, reliable, and user-friendly experience on our mobile application.

In compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and applicable rules, this Grievance Redressal Policy provides a mechanism for users to report complaints and seek resolution.

2. Grievance Officer

In accordance with applicable laws, we have appointed a Grievance Officer to address user concerns.

Grievance Officer Contact Details:

📧 Email: kagwad.in@gmail.com

3. Scope of Grievances

Users may raise complaints regarding:

  • Data privacy and security concerns
  • User account or authentication issues
  • App content, functionality, or technical errors
  • Any misuse, abuse, or policy violations
  • Any other service-related concerns

4. How to Submit a Grievance

You may submit your grievance by sending an email to the Grievance Officer.

To help us resolve your issue efficiently, please include:

  • Your full name and contact details
  • A detailed description of the issue
  • Relevant screenshots or supporting documents (if any)
  • Date and time of the incident (if applicable)

5. Grievance Redressal Process

Acknowledgement

We will acknowledge receipt of your grievance within 24 to 48 hours.

Review & Investigation

Your grievance will be reviewed and investigated by the concerned team.

Resolution Timeline

We aim to resolve all grievances within 15 days from the date of receipt.

In case of complex issues, resolution may take longer, and you will be informed accordingly.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Raise complaints without discrimination
  • Receive timely updates on grievance status
  • Seek clarification regarding resolution

7. Escalation Mechanism

If you are not satisfied with the resolution provided, you may escalate the matter by contacting us again at:

📧 Email: kagwad.in@gmail.com

(You may include additional details or request further review.)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Grievance Redressal Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

For general queries or support (non-grievance), you may contact us at:

📧 Email: kagwad.in@gmail.com
