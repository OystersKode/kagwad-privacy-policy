Community Guidelines

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kagwad.in. Our mission is to build a safe, respectful, and trustworthy platform for all users.

By accessing or using the Kagwad.in mobile application, you agree to comply with these Community Guidelines. These rules are designed to ensure a positive experience and maintain the integrity of our platform.

2. Respectful Behavior

We encourage open discussions and diverse opinions. However, the following behaviors are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment or Bullying: Any behavior that threatens, abuses, or harasses other users
  • Hate Speech: Content that promotes discrimination, hatred, or violence based on race, religion, gender, nationality, disability, or other protected characteristics
  • Violence or Harmful Content: Content that promotes violence, self-harm, or illegal activities

3. Content Standards

Users are responsible for the content they post. You must ensure that your content:

  • Is accurate and not misleading, especially for public or community-related information
  • Does not contain spam, repetitive posts, or unauthorized promotions
  • Respects intellectual property rights, including copyrights and trademarks
  • Is relevant to the purpose of the platform

4. Privacy and Safety

We are committed to protecting user privacy and safety:

  • Do not share sensitive personal information (e.g., phone numbers, addresses, IDs)
  • Respect the privacy of others at all times
  • Any content involving exploitation or harm to minors will result in immediate action and may be reported to authorities

5. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are not allowed on Kagwad.in:

  • Impersonation: Pretending to be another person, organization, or authority
  • Illegal Activities: Promoting or engaging in unlawful acts
  • Technical Misuse: Attempting to bypass security systems, scraping data, or distributing malicious software

6. Moderation and Enforcement

We actively monitor content using a combination of automated systems and manual review.

If a user violates these guidelines, we may take actions including:

  • Removal of content
  • Temporary suspension of account
  • Permanent account termination for severe or repeated violations

7. Reporting Violations

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines, you can:

  • Use the "Report" feature within the app
  • Contact us via email at: kagwad.in@gmail.com

We review all reports and take appropriate action.

8. User Responsibility

Users are solely responsible for the content they share on the platform. Kagwad.in is not responsible for user-generated content but reserves the right to remove any content that violates these guidelines.

9. Updates to Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or platform policies. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding these guidelines, please contact us:

📧 Email: kagwad.in@gmail.com

11. Acknowledgement

By using Kagwad.in, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to these Community Guidelines.
